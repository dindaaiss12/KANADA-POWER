The History Of (N)aturae

Naturae was founded on the belief that self-care is a right for everyone, without exception. Established in 2024, Naturae was born with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide inclusive, sustainable personal care services based on natural ingredients. We recognize that self-care is not just about the physical, but also about mental and emotional well-being. From hair care to body care, mental health to physical health, we design each service to support your holistic well-being.

Initially, we were inspired by the need for care services that are friendly to everyone, including those with disabilities. We understand that access to beauty and health services is often limited for many people. Therefore, Naturae emerged as a solution, offering services that can be accessed by everyone, whether at our clinic or in the comfort of your home. Our team is trained to provide compassionate and attentive care, especially for those who require extra comfort at home.

In addition to our services, we also offer a range of personal care products formulated from natural, safe, and sustainable ingredients. Each of our products is carefully designed, prioritizing quality and harmony with nature.

Since its inception, Naturae has continued to innovate in delivering the best services and products, staying true to the principles of inclusivity and the use of natural ingredients. We are proud to be part of your journey towards health and happiness, wherever you may be.
